HOW TO PLAY

SELECT YOUR ROLE TO GET STARTED

🎓 TEACHER

TEACHER'S GUIDE

01
Sign In
Go to the Teacher page and enter your staff email address. You'll receive a 6-digit verification code — enter it to access the dashboard. Your login stays active for 7 days.
✉️ Only authorised school email addresses are accepted
02
Choose a Game Mode
Pick Teams to have students compete in groups, or Individuals for every student to play for themselves. In Teams mode, set how many teams (2–8) and name each one.
👥 Teams mode is great for larger classes
03
Upload Questions
Download the Excel template, fill in your questions, answers and point values (default 2pts each), then upload it. You'll see a preview of the questions before starting.
📋 Up to any number of questions — they'll be shuffled randomly
04
Create a Room
Click Create Room to generate a 6-letter room code. Share this code with students so they can join. You can see players appear in the lobby list in real time. In Teams mode, wait until every player has selected a team before starting.
📋 Use "Copy" to quickly share the room code
05
Run the Game
Click Reveal Question to show a question to all students — it types out character by character and is read aloud. When a student buzzes in, the reveal pauses and you see who buzzed. Judge the spoken answer, then click Correct or Incorrect.
🎤 The question is read aloud automatically via text-to-speech
06
Correct vs Incorrect
A correct answer reveals the answer on screen, awards points, and moves to the next question. An incorrect answer deducts a point — the question gets shuffled back into the remaining questions to be answered later. Click Next → to continue.
🔄 Wrong answers come back later — no question is skipped forever
07
Game Over
Once every question has been correctly answered, the game ends and a leaderboard appears on everyone's screen. You can then choose to Play Again (sends everyone back to the lobby) or Go Home to close the room permanently.
🏆 Play Again reshuffles all questions for a fresh game
⚡ STUDENT

STUDENT'S GUIDE

01
Join the Room
Click Student on the home page, then enter the 6-letter room code your teacher gives you. Type your name and hit Join Game. You'll be taken straight to the lobby.
📱 Works on any phone, tablet or computer
02
Pick Your Team
If your teacher set up Teams mode, you'll be shown the available teams — tap one to join it. Your button will highlight to confirm your choice. In Individual mode, you'll go straight to a waiting screen.
⚠️ You must pick a team before the game can start
03
Listen to the Question
When the teacher reveals a question, it appears on your screen character by character and is read aloud. Read it carefully — you'll need to know the answer before buzzing in!
🔊 Make sure your volume is turned up
04
Buzz In!
When you know the answer, tap the big red BUZZ IN! button. Only the first person to buzz gets to answer — everyone else is locked out. The teacher will ask you to say your answer out loud.
⚡ Only buzz if you know the answer — a wrong answer costs a point!
05
Points & Scoring
Answer correctly and your team (or you) earns the question's points — usually 2. Answer incorrectly and you lose 1 point. The question comes back later for someone else to try. Your running score is shown at the top of your screen.
💡 Don't guess — it's better to let someone else buzz than lose a point
06
Game Over
When all questions are answered correctly, you'll see a Game Over screen and a leaderboard showing the final scores. Wait for your teacher — they'll either start a new game or end the session.
🏆 Top of the leaderboard wins!
